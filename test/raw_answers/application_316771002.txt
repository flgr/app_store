    <key>description</key><string>DSLR Camera Remote Professional Edition is the next-generation cable release for your Canon EOS&#169; or Nikon DSLR camera. Just connect your camera to a Wi-Fi enabled computer and you can instantly adjust its settings, fire the shutter, review images, even get a live viewfinder preview using your Apple iPhone or iPod Touch. DSLR Camera Remote is a must have for remote shooting applications like high or low angles, self-portraits and children. All you need is your DSLR and a Wi-Fi enabled computer to start shooting now.

--------------------
Important Note
--------------------

DSLR Camera Remote requires the use of the free server software on a Mac OSX or Windows host computer. You can download the server software from www.ononesoftware.com/iphone.

--------------------
Summary
--------------------

• Remotely fire your DSLR over any WiFi network
• Remotely control the camera settings like shutter-speed, aperture, white-balance, etc.
• See the images you have taken right on your iPhone or iPod Touch
• Look through and focus your cameras viewfinder remotely with LiveView preview on supported cameras
• Great for difficult camera angles where it is difficult to see through the viewfinder
• Use it for self-portraits, you can compose and review your shot without having to move to the camera
• Use it for remote firing at weddings and sporting events

-------------------
Key Features
-------------------

• Remotely fire your DSLR Camera
• Remotely adjust your DSLR Camera settings like White Balance, Shutter Speed, Aperture and Exposure Compensation
• Image Review lets you see the image you just photographed from your DSLR Camera on your iPhone
• LiveView mode lets you see through the camera&#39;s viewfinder right on your iPhone or iPod Touch (requires the use of a supported camera)
• Intervalometer – This lets you create a custom self-timer or take up to 999 shots from as little as 1 second apart to up to 1 day apart.
• Auto Bracketing mode for expanded exposure brackets, perfect for HDR.
• Burst Mode for continuous shooting.
